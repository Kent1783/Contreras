ABOUT KENT

Influential, Further reinforcing its position as one of the world’s most desirable fashion houses. Eclectic, contemporary, romantic—KENT products represent the pinnacle of Italian craftsmanship and are unsurpassed for their quality and attention to detail. KENT is part of the Kering Group. A global Luxury group, Kering manages the development of a series of renowned Houses in Fashion, Leather Goods, Jewelry and Watches.
